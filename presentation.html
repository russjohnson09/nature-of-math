Twin Primes

Definition of Twin Primes

If $p$ and $p+2$ are both prime numbers, then $p$ and $p+2$ make up a pair of twin primes.

Definition of Chen Prime

A prime number p is called a Chen prime if p + 2 is either a prime or a product of two primes.
